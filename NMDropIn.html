Drop-In

Eric Sanders - Tutorial Coordinator

The mission of the Tutorial Center is to provide comprehensive academic support services to assist students in developing and strengthening the knowledge and skills needed to reach their academic goals.

The primary function of the Tutorial Center is to provide peer assisted learning. Peer facilitation/Tutoring promotes independence in learning and provides needed academic assistance to students. Independence in learning is fostered in a tutoring environment by engaging students with effective questions. The center's tutoring philosophy is "questions before answers." The types and frequency of questions tutors ask, therefore, are more important than the asweres they give. Tutors guide, coach, encourage, and model how successful students excel in college.

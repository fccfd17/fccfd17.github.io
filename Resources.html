Front-Desk

Front Desk is the first part of the tutorial center you will encounter. The front desk staff are dedicated to helping students check-in and check-out of the Tutorial Center. The Front Desk staff are committed to helping students with their questions of tutor schedules, student log hours, locations of departments, and providing study materials for students.

Items for Check-Out

  • Laptops
  • Calculators
  • White Boards
  • Textbooks
  • Mouse for Laptop

Please note that if you wish to check out an item, you must be a student and register with the tutorial center. If you have any questions or concers, please contact the Tutorial Center at fccfd17@gmail.com or +1 (559) 442-8209

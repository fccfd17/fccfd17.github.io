Math Drop-In

Heather Walker - Math Coordinator

The Math Success program provides a variety of services for students seeking help with their math classes. Extending the Class (ETC) is a program where an ETC Leader is partnered with one instructor, attends classes, and conducts weekly study sessions specifically for students in that particular class. Study sessions give students the opportunity to actively engage with their peers from the class on course related material including homework, helpful study guides, worksheets, and practice exams. ETC is available for many math courses. In addition to ETC, there are also drop-in tutoring services available for math students. Math tutors are staffed during all open hours of operation and students are welcome to visit the Tutorial Center and work one-on-one with a certified, trained tutor. Also available to students is our Group Assistance Program (GRASP) where tutors partner with math instructors to assist students with their math needs. Students do not need to schedule an appointment to attend GRASP, they can simply drop by and work with instructors and tutors.

We realize that math can be intimidating to students, but we offer many FREE services to help alleviate math anxiety! We are here to help!
